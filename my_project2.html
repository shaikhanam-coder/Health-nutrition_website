Health & Nutrition Awareness

Stay Healthy . Eat Well . Live Better

1.Importance of Healthy Lifestyle

A healthy lifestyle helps us maintain physical and mental fitness. Eating nutritious food, drinking enough water, and exercising regularly can prevent many diseases and improve our quality of life.

3.Do’s and Don’ts for Good Health

Do’s

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily
  • Eat fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Exercise regularly
  • Get proper sleep

Don’ts

  • Avoid junk food
  • Do not skip meals
  • Avoid excessive sugar and oil
  • Do not stay awake late at night

4. Basic Health Tips

  1. Start your day with warm water
  2. Wash hands before eating
  3. Maintain personal hygiene
  4. Manage stress through meditation
